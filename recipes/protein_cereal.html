Protein Packed Cereal Recipe

So it appears that you want to know the best kept secret to hit your daily macros. Observe a high protein breakfast that is extremely low effort.

Necessary Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Milk
  • 1 scoop of Protein Powder
  • 1 cup of Cereal
  • Blender or Shaker bottle

Instructions on how to make Powerful Protein Packed Cereal

  1. First add a scoop of protein powder and 1 cup of milk to a blender/shaker
  2. Mix the protein shake well, until smooth
  3. Add one cup of cereal to a bowl
  4. Add protein shake to bowl of cereal
    Note: NEVER ADD PROTEIN SHAKE BEFORE CEREAL teehee
  5. Enjoy your delicious protein packed breakfast
